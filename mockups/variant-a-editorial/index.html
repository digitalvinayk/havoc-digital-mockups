Honest answers

Frequently asked questions.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Realistically: 4–6 months for new sites in low-competition niches, 9–12 months for established sites entering competitive markets, and 12–18 months for national-scale dominance. Anyone promising rankings in 30 days is selling something else.

Do you guarantee specific rankings?

No. No legitimate agency does — Google has explicitly stated that ranking guarantees are a red flag. We do guarantee process: audits delivered, content shipped, links earned, and monthly reporting on rankings, organic traffic, and revenue attribution.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Discovery call → audit phase (2–4 weeks) → 90-day priority plan → monthly delivery cycle → quarterly strategy review. We work on rolling 90-day commitments rather than 12-month lock-ins because we'd rather earn renewal each quarter than hide behind a contract.

How do you report on results?

Weekly Slack updates on activity. Monthly reports covering rankings, traffic, conversions, and revenue attribution where tracking allows. Quarterly business reviews with the principal stakeholder to recalibrate strategy.

How do you choose between SEO, Google Ads, and Social?

The right channel mix depends on intent, audience location, sales cycle, and unit economics. Default starting recommendation: SEO for compounding long-term value, Google Ads for testable demand at scale, social for retargeting and brand familiarity. We won't push services that don't fit your business.

Why work with a boutique agency instead of a large one?

Large agencies sell senior strategy and deliver junior execution. Boutique agencies deliver senior execution. We have 14 years of accumulated pattern recognition and bring it to every engagement. We turn down work we can't do well.

DHL EXPRESS

AI search campaign for DHL: +287% organic traffic.

DHL needed to defend category share against newer logistics entrants on AI search. We rebuilt their topic clusters around entity coverage, deployed schema across 47 priority pages, and ran a 12-piece thought leadership program. Six months in: organic traffic up 287%, AI Overview citations up 1,420%, and three target keywords moving from page two to position one.

18 months. E-commerce / logistics.

RAVE

Capturing demand from ChatGPT referrals.

RAVE wanted to capture demand from AI assistant queries before competitors did.

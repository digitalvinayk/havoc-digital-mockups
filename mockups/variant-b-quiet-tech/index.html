FAQ

Honest answers to the questions every prospect asks.

How long does SEO take to show results?

4–6 months for new sites in low-competition niches. 9–12 months for established sites entering competitive markets. 12–18 months for national-scale dominance. Anyone promising rankings in 30 days is selling something else.

Do you guarantee specific rankings?

No. No legitimate agency does — Google has explicitly stated that ranking guarantees are a red flag. We do guarantee process: audits delivered, content shipped, links earned, monthly reporting on rankings, traffic, and revenue.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Discovery call → 2–4 week audit → 90-day priority plan → monthly delivery cycle → quarterly strategy review. Rolling 90-day commitments rather than 12-month lock-ins.

How do you report on results?

Weekly Slack updates on activity. Monthly reports covering rankings, traffic, conversions, and revenue attribution. Quarterly business reviews with the principal stakeholder.

How do you choose between SEO, Google Ads, and Social?

The right channel mix depends on intent, audience location, sales cycle, and unit economics. SEO for compounding long-term value, Google Ads for testable demand at scale, social for retargeting and brand familiarity.

Why work with a boutique agency instead of a large one?

Large agencies sell senior strategy and deliver junior execution. Boutique agencies deliver senior execution. We bring 14 years of accumulated pattern recognition to every engagement.
